Skill India Mission

Ministry: Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship  
Department: Department of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship  
Launch Date: 15 July 2015  
Type: National Skill Development Initiative  

--------------------------------------------------

1. Overview:

Skill India Mission is a national initiative aimed at equipping Indian youth with employable skills through vocational training, certification, and industry alignment.

The mission integrates various sub-schemes including:

• Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)  
• Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)  
• Apprenticeship Training Scheme  
• National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF)  
• Sector Skill Councils (SSCs)  

Objectives:

• Bridge the skill gap.
• Improve workforce productivity.
• Increase employability.
• Promote entrepreneurship.

--------------------------------------------------

2. Benefits:

1. Free Skill Training Programs (under PMKVY).
2. Industry-recognized certification.
3. Placement assistance support.
4. Stipend for apprentices under Apprenticeship Act.
5. Recognition of informal skills via RPL.
6. Soft skills and entrepreneurship training.

--------------------------------------------------

3. Eligibility:

• Indian citizens.
• Age criteria varies by course.
• Educational qualification varies by training level.

Special Focus Groups:

• School dropouts.
• Rural youth.
• Women.
• SC/ST/OBC communities.
• Persons with disabilities.

Exclusions:

• Individuals not meeting age or qualification criteria for specific course.

--------------------------------------------------

4. Application Process:

Step 1:
Visit Skill India portal.

Step 2:
Register with personal details.

Step 3:
Select preferred course and training center.

Step 4:
Document verification.

Step 5:
Enroll in training program.

Step 6:
Complete course and assessment.

Step 7:
Receive certification.

--------------------------------------------------

5. Required Documents:

• Aadhaar Card
• Educational qualification proof
• Bank account details
• Passport-size photographs

--------------------------------------------------

6. Frequently Asked Questions:

Q1: Is training free?
A: Many programs under PMKVY are free.

Q2: Do I get a certificate?
A: Yes, after successful assessment.

Q3: Is placement guaranteed?
A: Placement assistance is provided but not guaranteed.

Q4: What is RPL?
A: Recognition of Prior Learning for informal workers.

Q5: Is there age limit?
A: Depends on specific course.
