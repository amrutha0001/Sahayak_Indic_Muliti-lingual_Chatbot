Swachh Bharat Mission - Grameen (Phase II)

Ministry: Ministry of Jal Shakti  
Department: Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation  
Launch Date: 2020  
Type: Centrally Sponsored Rural Sanitation Scheme  

--------------------------------------------------

1. Overview:

Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) Phase II [SBM(G) Phase II] focuses on sustaining the Open Defecation Free (ODF) status achieved under Phase I and strengthening Solid and Liquid Waste Management (SLWM) systems in rural India.

The programme aims to ensure that no one is left behind and that everyone uses a toilet. Phase II is implemented in mission mode from 2020–21 to 2025–26.

The key objective is to make villages ODF Plus through:

- ODF Sustainability
- Solid Waste Management
- Liquid Waste Management
- Visual Cleanliness

--------------------------------------------------

2. Benefits:

1. Construction of Individual Household Latrines (IHHLs):
   Financial assistance of Rs. 12,000 for eligible rural households for toilet construction.

2. Retrofitting of Toilets:
   Technological upgrades to improve functionality of existing toilets.

3. Construction of Community Sanitary Complexes (CSCs):
   - At least one CSC in ODF Plus villages with more than 100 households.
   - Priority to SC/ST areas and migrant worker locations.

4. Solid Waste Management:
   - Compost pits
   - Waste segregation systems

5. Liquid Waste Management:
   - Soak pits for greywater
   - Stormwater management systems

6. Faecal Sludge Management (FSM):
   Implementation in peri-urban or densely populated rural areas.

7. Operation and Maintenance:
   Managed by Gram Panchayats for decentralized sanitation asset management.

Other Activities:

- IEC campaigns
- Capacity building initiatives
- Swachh Survekshan Grameen (SSG) Phase II
- Swachhata Action Plan (SAP)
- Swachh Iconic Places (SIP)
- Start-up Grand Challenge
- National awareness campaigns

--------------------------------------------------

3. Eligibility:

Eligible households must:

- Not have a toilet.
- Not have received toilet construction benefits under any other government programme.

Priority Order for Incentives:

1. Below Poverty Line (BPL) households.
2. Above Poverty Line (APL) households in the following order:
   - SC/ST households
   - Persons with disabilities (divyangjan friendly toilets ensured)
   - Landless labourers with homestead
   - Small farmers
   - Marginal farmers
   - Women-headed households

Applications are subject to verification and approval by block/district authorities.

Only approved applicants receive incentives.

--------------------------------------------------

4. Application Process:

Mode: Online and Offline

Registration Process:

Step 1:
Visit the official SBM(G) website.

Step 2:
Click on “Application Form for IHHL” under Citizen Corner.

Step 3:
Register by entering mobile number and verifying via OTP.

Step 4:
Fill personal details including:
- Name
- Gender
- Address
- State
- Captcha code

Step 5:
Submit registration.

Login and Application:

Step 1:
Login using registered mobile number and OTP.

Step 2:
Click on “New Application” under Modules section.

Step 3:
Complete application form.

Step 4:
Upload required documents.

Step 5:
Submit application.

Applications are verified by concerned authorities before approval.

6. Frequently Asked Questions (Selected):

Q: What is SBM(G) Phase II?
A: A rural sanitation programme focused on sustaining ODF status and implementing solid and liquid waste management systems.

Q: What is the financial support for IHHL construction?
A: Rs. 12,000 per eligible household.

Q: Who is eligible for incentives?
A: Rural households without toilets who have not previously received government assistance for toilet construction.

Q: How are applications approved?
A: Applications are verified by block/district authorities before approval.
