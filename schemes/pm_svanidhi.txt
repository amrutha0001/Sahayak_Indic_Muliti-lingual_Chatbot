PM Street Vendor’s AtmaNirbhar Nidhi (PM SVANidhi)

Ministry: Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs  
Department: Department of Housing & Urban Affairs  
Launch Date: 1 June 2020  
Type: Central Sector Micro-Credit Scheme  

--------------------------------------------------

1. Overview:

PM Street Vendor’s AtmaNirbhar Nidhi (PM SVANidhi) is a central sector micro-credit scheme launched to provide collateral-free working capital loans to street vendors.

The scheme supports vendors affected by the COVID-19 pandemic and aims to:

- Provide affordable working capital loans.
- Promote digital transactions.
- Formalize street vendors within the urban economy.
- Enable vendors to move up the economic ladder.

Loan Structure:

- First Loan: Rs. 10,000 (collateral-free)
- Second Loan: Rs. 20,000 (on timely repayment of first loan)
- Third Loan: Rs. 50,000 (on timely repayment of second loan)
- Interest Subsidy: 7%
- Digital transaction incentive: Cashback up to Rs. 100 per month.

Street vendors include hawkers, thelewala, rehriwala, and small service providers selling goods such as vegetables, fruits, food items, apparel, footwear, and offering services like barbering, cobbling, laundry, etc.

--------------------------------------------------

2. Benefits:

- Working capital loan up to Rs. 10,000 initially.
- Subsequent enhanced loans of Rs. 20,000 and Rs. 50,000.
- 7% interest subsidy on timely repayment.
- No collateral required.
- No processing fee.
- Cashback incentive for digital transactions (up to Rs. 100 per month).
- One-year repayment period.
- Nationwide availability across all States and Union Territories.
- Supports entrepreneurship and employment generation.

--------------------------------------------------

3. Eligibility:

Eligible beneficiaries include:

1. Street vendors with Certificate of Vending (CoV) or Identity Card issued by Urban Local Bodies (ULBs).

2. Vendors identified in survey but not issued CoV/ID.

3. Vendors left out of survey or who started vending after survey completion and issued Letter of Recommendation (LoR) by ULB or Town Vending Committee (TVC).

4. Vendors from peri-urban or rural areas vending within ULB limits and issued LoR by ULB/TVC.

--------------------------------------------------

4. Application Process:

Mode: Online and Offline

Online Application:

Step 1:
Visit official PM SVANidhi portal.

Step 2:
Click “Log In”.

Step 3:
Enter mobile number and captcha; request OTP.

Step 4:
Select vendor category.

Step 5:
Enter Survey Reference Number (SRN).

Step 6:
Fill application form.

Step 7:
Upload required documents.

Step 8:
Submit application.

For assistance:
Toll-Free Helpline: 1800111979 (9:30 AM – 6:00 PM, Monday to Saturday)

--------------------------------------------------

5. Documents Required:

For First Loan:

Category A & B Vendors:
- Certificate of Vending
- Identity Card

Category C & D Vendors:
- Letter of Recommendation (LoR)

KYC Documents (as applicable):
- Aadhaar Card
- Voter ID
- Driving License
- MNREGA Card
- PAN Card

For Letter of Recommendation:
- Account statement or passbook copy
- Membership card or proof of vendor status
- Request letter to ULB
- Any document supporting vendor claim

For Second Loan:
- Loan closure document of previous loan

--------------------------------------------------

6. Frequently Asked Questions (Selected):

Q: What is the maximum loan amount under PM SVANidhi?
A: Initial loan of Rs. 10,000, followed by Rs. 20,000 and Rs. 50,000 on successful repayment.

Q: What is the interest rate?
A: 7% interest subsidy is provided on timely repayment.

Q: Is collateral required?
A: No. The loan is collateral-free.

Q: Is there any processing fee?
A: No processing fee is charged.

Q: What is the repayment period?
A: One year.

Q: Can the loan be used for any purpose?
A: It must be used for purposes related to street vending business such as working capital, inventory, equipment, etc.
